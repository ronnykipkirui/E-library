Welcome to the Maktaba Online Library


A service dedicated to students, Distance learning community. We provide online resources, professional support and guidance to all our students whenever, and from wherever they have chosen to study.

You can also browse our databases, resources and the A-Z journals list. If you need help, read our Summon Quick Start Guide. Log into Refworks, the Online Library's reference management tool. To set up your RefWorks account and learn how to use it, see our reference management guide. The Online Library must be used within copyright law. Do not share your password or any materials in the library with other people. This could lead to the removal of your access to the library.
